Ka'ali

  • Race: Half-elf
  • Class: Rogue, Assassin

"Name's Ka'ali, and there's not a pocket I can't pick, a lock I can't get around, or a pair of shackles I can't get out of (apparently, there isn't a pair of shackles that I can avoid either, but we won't get into that). At any given day, you'll find me either taking the fight to trees, the real evil in this world, with Rinnor, or in the tavern running grifts with my buddy Puck or getting cold-clocked by Azhian. And, remember, if anything goes wrong.... It was Bomiz."


Puck

"Ex-merchant submariner satyr looking for a good story to tell with his ukulele. Enjoys long crawls on the beach to avoid birds, takes pride in every curse word he teaches Bomiz."

"Praise be to Flombax. May He bless our journey and wrap us with His infinite tentacles to keep us warm in the cold of night."
